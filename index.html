Kings Island Itinerary

About

This is a itinerary for what I will do when I visit Kings Island. Kings Island is an amusement park located in Mason, Ohio, and is the closest amusement park to Cincinnati. It features a map of the park with a placemark indicating where the park is located. I have always wanted to go to Kings Island, and I can't wait until I eventually go there soon.

Guide

Tickets

Tickets would be purchased on the Kings Island Park Website. To search tickets, click on the menu section that says "Tickets & Passes". The section features stuff like Admission, Upgrades & More, and Information. The Admission features stuff like Season Pass, Daily Tickets, Groups, and Military. Tickets can also be won via companies that do offer them such as Toyota or AAA.

Hotels

There are several hotels near Kings Island. For Hilton, the closest Hilton branded hotels next to Kings Island are Tru by Hilton and Hampton Inn. The Great Wolf Lodge, a indoor water park resort that is one of many indoor water park resorts, is located next to the amusement park. For a list of other hotels located near the park, click here.

Rides and attractions

Kings Island has many notable rides and attractions. It has like 14 coasters, with the newest being the Snoopy's Soap Box Racers, a family Boomerang. The park also features a Eiffel Tower replica, where you can view the park from. To view a list of the attractions, click on this link on the park web page. Another way you can do it on the web page is to go through the "Rides & Experiences" page, which will show features such as Rides, Soak City Water Park, Special Events, Dining, Live Entertainment, Cabanas, Shopping, and Seasons of Fun.

Soak City

Soak City is a water park located at Kings Island. It includes a total of 50 water activities, including 36 water slides.
