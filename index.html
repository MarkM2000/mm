Kings Island Itinerary

About

This is a itinerary for what I will do when I visit Kings Island. Kings Island is a amusement park located in Mason, Ohio, and is the closest amusement park to Cincinnati. It features a map of the park with a placemark indicating where the park is located. I have always wanted to go to Kings Island, and I can't wait until I eventually go there soon.
